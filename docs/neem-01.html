About this Tree

The neem tree is native to the Indian subcontinent and has been used in traditional medicine (Ayurveda) for thousands of years. Nearly every part of the tree - leaves, bark, seeds and oil - has a traditional or modern use, from skincare to natural pest control.

Did You Know?

  • Neem oil is a natural, biodegradable pesticide used in organic farming.
  • Neem twigs have traditionally been used as toothbrushes (datun).
  • A mature neem tree can absorb a significant amount of CO2 and air pollutants.
  • Neem trees can survive in poor soil and drought conditions.

Uses & Benefits

  • Traditional medicine and skincare
  • Natural pesticide and pest repellent
  • Shade tree and soil improvement
  • Timber for furniture
